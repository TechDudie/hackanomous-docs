# Welcome to the Hackanomous Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for **Hackanomous**!

Hackanomous is a **Hack Club YSWS (You Ship, We Ship)** programme where teenagers aged **13–18** design, build, and ship AI-powered projects. Whether you're creating software, hardware, or something completely original, Hackanomous is here to help you turn your idea into reality.

As you build and ship projects, you'll earn **Bolts**, which can be redeemed for exciting rewards such as hardware, AI credits, development resources, and more.

---

## What is Hackanomous?

Our mission is simple:

> **Build projects using AI that solve real-world problems.**

Hackanomous isn't about adding AI just because it's trendy. We encourage you to think critically, explore where AI genuinely adds value, and create projects that make a real difference.

Whether you're:

- Building software
- Creating hardware
- Learning about AI for the first time
- Shipping your first major project

there's a place for you here.

## What you'll learn

Throughout Hackanomous you'll learn how to:

- Generate and refine project ideas
- Build practical AI-powered applications
- Use AI responsibly and effectively
- Ship complete software and hardware projects
- Showcase your work to the community

---

## Rewards

Participants earn **Bolts** by building and shipping projects.

Bolts can be exchanged for a range of rewards, including:

- Raspberry Pi grants
- AI API credits
- Development hardware
- RAM and GPU grants
- And many more exciting rewards throughout the programme

---

## Need help?

Don't worry if you're completely new to AI or programming.

Hackanomous is designed to help you learn by building, with documentation, guides, and a community ready to help you along the way.
